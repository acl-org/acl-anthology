Title: How can I stay up to date with the ACL Anthology?
weight: 2
---
You may be interested in the following:
- We use our [email-based announcement list](http://groups.google.com/group/acl-anthology?hl=en) for ingestion of major proceedings and other significant updates
- We have limited social media presence on [X](https://x.com/aclanthology) and [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/aclanthology.org)
- The [RSS feed]({{ "posts/index.xml" | relURL }}) contains all news posts, including ingestion of major proceedings and other significant updates
- Another [RSS feed]({{ "papers/index.xml" | relURL }}) contains the latest 1000 papers added to the Anthology
